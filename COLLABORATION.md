# Research collaboration — terms (read before we partner)

This project is offered for **independent, non-commercial research collaboration**. It is designed so a
vendor can co-author credible, third-party evidence about their AI without any data-sharing or IP friction.

## What I bring
- A **100% synthetic** GxP substrate (SOPs, dossiers in two jurisdictions, deviation/CAPA + batch data),
- Pre-built **golden benchmarks**: 50-scenario risk-classification bank (+ blinded human-rating sheet),
  CAPA-adequacy set, document-heterogeneity set, deviation seeded-defect sets,
- A **deterministic, reproducible** risk/validation method + a validate-the-validator (κ) protocol.

## How a collaboration works
1. You run **your tool** on the shared **synthetic** cases and return outputs (`l7/vendor_output_template.csv`).
2. We compute agreement vs golden + (where available) human raters, and analyze gaps.
3. We **co-author** a short paper / poster. You get independent third-party evidence + credibility; I get authorship + the research.

## Terms (non-negotiable, and they protect both of us)
- **Synthetic / public data only.** No proprietary, employer, client, or patient data on either side.
- **Non-commercial.** No fees, no paid consulting/advisory, no sponsorship. This is research co-authorship only.
- **Co-authorship with consent.** All contributors named with permission; honest authorship order.
- **Vendor-neutral & independent.** Findings reported honestly, including negative results; no endorsement implied.
- **License:** code MIT; written outputs co-owned by the authors.

*The maintainer participates in a personal capacity; this is not sponsored or endorsed by any employer.
Formal agreements will be reviewed by appropriate counsel before signature.*
